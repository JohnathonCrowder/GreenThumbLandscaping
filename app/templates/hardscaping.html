Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical hardscaping project take?

Project duration varies depending on the scope and complexity, but most projects are completed within 2-6 weeks from design approval to final installation.

What materials do you use for hardscaping?

We use a variety of high-quality materials including natural stone, pavers, concrete, and brick. We'll help you choose the best options for your project and budget.

Can hardscaping help with drainage issues?

Yes, proper hardscape design and installation can significantly improve drainage in your outdoor space, preventing water accumulation and potential damage.

Do you offer eco-friendly hardscaping options?

Absolutely! We offer permeable paving options and use sustainable materials when possible to minimize environmental impact.

How do I maintain my new hardscape features?

Most hardscape elements are low-maintenance. We provide care instructions and offer maintenance services to keep your hardscaping looking great for years to come.

Can you integrate hardscaping with existing landscaping?

Yes, we specialize in creating seamless transitions between hardscape elements and your existing landscape, ensuring a cohesive and beautiful outdoor space.
