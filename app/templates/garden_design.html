Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the garden design process take?

The duration varies depending on the project's complexity, but typically ranges from 2-6 weeks for design creation, followed by implementation.

Can you work with my existing landscape?

Absolutely! We can incorporate existing elements into new designs or refresh your current landscape to enhance its beauty and functionality.

Do you offer eco-friendly garden designs?

Yes, we prioritize sustainable practices in our designs, including native plant selection, water-efficient irrigation, and environmentally friendly materials.

What if I don't know much about plants?

Our experts will guide you through plant selection, considering your preferences, maintenance requirements, and local climate to ensure a thriving garden.

Can you help with small garden spaces?

Definitely! We specialize in maximizing the potential of gardens of all sizes, creating beautiful and functional spaces even in compact areas.

Do you provide ongoing maintenance after installation?

Yes, we offer maintenance plans to keep your new garden looking its best. We can tailor these services to your specific needs and schedule.
