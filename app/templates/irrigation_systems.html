Frequently Asked Questions

How much water can I save with a proper irrigation system?

A well-designed, smart irrigation system can reduce water usage by 30-50% compared to manual watering, while maintaining a healthier landscape.

How often should I have my irrigation system serviced?

We recommend a professional check-up at least twice a year - in spring to ensure proper startup, and in fall to prepare for winter.

Can you upgrade my existing irrigation system?

Yes, we can assess your current system and recommend upgrades to improve efficiency, such as adding smart controllers or replacing outdated components.

Are your irrigation systems environmentally friendly?

Absolutely. We prioritize water conservation and use EPA WaterSense certified products when possible to ensure environmental responsibility.

How long does it take to install an irrigation system?

Installation time varies depending on the size and complexity of your landscape, but most residential systems can be installed in 1-3 days.

Can irrigation systems be used for both residential and commercial properties?

Yes, we design and install irrigation systems for properties of all sizes, from small residential gardens to large commercial landscapes.
