About Green Thumb Landscaping

Transforming outdoor spaces with passion and expertise since 2005

Our Story

Green Thumb Landscaping was founded in 2005 by married couple John and Emma Brooks, who shared a lifelong passion for gardening and environmental stewardship. Their journey began when they purchased their first home and transformed its neglected backyard into a thriving garden oasis, drawing admiration from neighbors and friends alike.

Inspired by their success and driven by a desire to help others create beautiful, sustainable outdoor spaces, John and Emma decided to turn their passion into a business. What started as a small, local lawn care service has grown into a full-service landscaping company, serving residential and commercial clients throughout the greater Philadelphia area.

Today, Green Thumb Landscaping is proud to be a leader in innovative, eco-friendly landscaping solutions. John's background in horticulture and Emma's expertise in sustainable design have been the cornerstone of our approach. As we continue to grow and evolve, we remain true to our roots and our commitment to creating beautiful, environmentally responsible outdoor environments for our clients.

Our Mission

At Green Thumb Landscaping, our mission is to enhance the beauty and functionality of outdoor spaces while promoting environmental responsibility. We strive to:

  • Create stunning, sustainable landscapes that reflect our clients' unique styles and needs
  • Implement eco-friendly practices and materials in all our projects
  • Educate our clients and community about the importance of environmental stewardship
  • Provide exceptional customer service and build lasting relationships with our clients
  • Foster a positive, growth-oriented work environment for our team members
