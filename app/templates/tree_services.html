Frequently Asked Questions

How often should trees be pruned?

The frequency of pruning depends on the tree species, age, and health. Generally, mature trees benefit from pruning every 3-5 years, while younger trees may need more frequent attention.

When is the best time to plant new trees?

The ideal planting time is typically in the fall or early spring when temperatures are cooler and rainfall is more frequent, giving new trees the best chance to establish strong root systems.

How can I tell if my tree is diseased?

Signs of tree disease include discolored or distorted leaves, premature leaf drop, bark abnormalities, fungal growth, and dead branches. If you notice any of these symptoms, it's best to consult with our arborists for a proper diagnosis.

Is it necessary to remove tree stumps?

While not always necessary, stump removal is recommended to prevent regrowth, eliminate trip hazards, and improve the appearance of your landscape. It also helps prevent pest infestations that can spread to healthy trees.

How do you ensure safety during tree removal?

Our team follows strict safety protocols, uses professional-grade equipment, and employs advanced techniques to ensure safe tree removal. We also carry comprehensive insurance for your peace of mind.

Can you help with emergency tree services?

Yes, we offer 24/7 emergency tree services for situations like storm damage or fallen trees. Our rapid response team is equipped to handle urgent tree care needs safely and efficiently.
