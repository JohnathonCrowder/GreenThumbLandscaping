Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I mow my lawn?

The frequency of mowing depends on your grass type and growing conditions. Generally, mowing once a week during the growing season is recommended.

What's the best time to fertilize my lawn?

The optimal time for fertilization varies by region and grass type. We typically recommend fertilizing in early spring, late spring, and fall for most lawns.

How can I prevent weeds in my lawn?

Regular mowing, proper watering, and maintaining a healthy, dense lawn are key to preventing weeds. We also offer targeted weed control treatments as part of our lawn care services.

Do you use eco-friendly products?

Yes, we prioritize the use of environmentally friendly products and practices in all our lawn care services to protect both your lawn and the environment.

How often should I water my lawn?

Watering frequency depends on various factors including climate, grass type, and soil conditions. Generally, deep, infrequent watering (1-2 times per week) is better than frequent, shallow watering.

Can you help with lawn pest infestations?

Absolutely. We offer comprehensive pest control services to identify and treat various lawn pests, using methods that are effective and safe for your family and pets.
